Walk onto the elevator
Review the numbers on the elevator Dashboard
Determine which numbers are lit and which are dim
After review, determine if the floor number you are headed to is lit or dim
	If your floor number is lit, take no action. 
	Otherwise:
		Press the dim button that corresponds with the floor you are headed to
	Once you have reached the floor number of your destination, leave the elevator.
